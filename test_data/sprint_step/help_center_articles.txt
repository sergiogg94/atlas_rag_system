ARTICLE: CREATING AN ACCOUNT
Category: Getting Started | Updated: Feb 2024

**Do I need an account to shop?**
No, you can check out as a guest. However, having an account lets you:
- Track orders in real-time
- Save favorite items
- Get faster checkout (saved payment info)
- Earn loyalty rewards
- Access VIP member perks (free shipping, extended returns)

**How do I create an account?**
1. Click "Sign Up" at top right of any page
2. Enter email and create password (8+ characters)
3. Add shipping address (optional but speeds up checkout)
4. Verify email (check inbox, click confirmation link)

**Forgot password?**
Click "Forgot Password" on login page. We'll email a reset link. Check spam folder if you don't see it within 5 minutes.

**Can I use social login?**
Yes! Sign up with Google, Facebook, or Apple ID for faster registration.

ARTICLE: PLACING AN ORDER
Category: Shopping | Updated: Jan 2024

**How do I place an order?**
1. Browse and add items to cart (click "Add to Cart")
2. Click cart icon (top right) to review
3. Click "Checkout"
4. Enter shipping address
5. Choose shipping speed
6. Enter payment information
7. Review and confirm order

**What payment methods do you accept?**
- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover
- Debit cards with Visa/Mastercard logo
- PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Shop Pay (installment payments available)
- Gift cards and store credit

**Do you accept international orders?**
Currently, we ship only within the United States (including Alaska, Hawaii, and Puerto Rico). APO/FPO addresses accepted.

**Can I modify or cancel my order?**
Yes, but act fast! You have 1 hour after placing order to modify or cancel (log into account > Orders > Select order > "Modify" or "Cancel"). After that, the order is in our warehouse being picked and packed.

**Didn't receive order confirmation?**
Check spam/promotions folder first. If nothing, contact us with email used at checkout – we'll resend it.

ARTICLE: SHIPPING & DELIVERY  
Category: Shipping | Updated: Feb 2024

**Shipping Options:**

| Speed | Timeframe | Cost |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Standard | 5-7 business days | FREE on $75+ / $6.95 under $75 |
| Expedited | 2-3 business days | $12.95 |
| Express | 1-2 business days | $19.95 |

**Do you offer free shipping?**
Yes! Standard shipping is free on orders $75 or more. VIP members get free shipping on ALL orders, no minimum.

**When will my order ship?**
Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship same day (Monday-Friday). Weekend orders ship Monday.

**How do I track my order?**
You'll receive tracking number via email once package ships. Click the link or paste number into tracking page on our website. Updates typically within 24 hours of shipment.

**My tracking hasn't updated in days. What's wrong?**
Tracking can sometimes lag, especially over weekends. If no update after 3 business days from ship date, contact us – we'll investigate with the carrier.

**What if I'm not home for delivery?**
Carrier will leave package at door (signature not required for most orders). If you live in apartment, they'll follow building's delivery instructions. Need signature delivery? Add it at checkout for $4.95 extra.

**Can I change my shipping address after ordering?**
Only within first hour of placing order (see "Modify Order" above). After that, contact us immediately – if we catch it before shipment, we can update it. Once shipped, you'll need to contact the carrier directly or wait for delivery and refuse package.

ARTICLE: RETURNS & EXCHANGES
Category: Returns | Updated: Oct 2023 (Policy v2.1)

**What is your return policy?**
60 days to return from delivery date (90 days for VIP members). Shoes must be unworn or lightly worn. We provide free return shipping.

**How do I start a return?**
1. Log into account > Orders
2. Select item > "Start Return"
3. Choose reason (helps us improve!)
4. Print prepaid return label  
5. Pack item securely (original box preferred but not required)
6. Drop off at USPS, UPS, or FedEx

**Can I return without a receipt?**
Yes! We can look up your order with email address or order number. Gift recipients can return for store credit.

**When will I get my refund?**
3-5 business days after we receive your return. You'll get email confirmation when refund processes. Bank may take additional 1-3 days to post to account.

**What about exchanges?**
We'll ship your exchange as soon as we receive your return – no need to wait for refund and reorder! Choose "Exchange" option when starting return.

**Can I return sale items?**
Yes, unless marked "Final Sale" at time of purchase. Final Sale items are clearly labeled and you'll see warning at checkout.

**What if shoes are defective?**
Contact us immediately with photos. We'll send replacement right away – no need to return defective pair first. Manufacturing defects covered 100%.

**I wore them once and they hurt. Can I return?**
Yes! We understand you need to test shoes. Light wear (indoors or one short run) is acceptable for returns. Just make sure they're clean and in resellable condition.

ARTICLE: SIZING & FIT
Category: Product Info | Updated: Jan 2024

**How do I find my size?**
Check our size chart (link in product description). Measure both feet – go with larger measurement. Sizes vary by brand, so always check specific product's size chart.

**Should I size up or down?**
General rule: running shoes should have thumb's width (about 1/2 inch) between longest toe and end of shoe. If between sizes, go up for running/training shoes, go down for casual/lifestyle sneakers.

**What if size chart doesn't match what I usually wear?**
Shoe sizing isn't standardized across brands. Always measure your foot and use that brand's specific size chart. When in doubt, check reviews – customers often mention if shoe runs large/small.

**Do you offer wide or narrow widths?**
Yes! Look for width options in size selector (where available). Most brands offer:
- N or B: Narrow
- M or D: Standard/Medium
- W or 2E: Wide  
- XW or 4E: Extra Wide

**What's your most popular size?**
Men's: 10-10.5 | Women's: 8-8.5 (but everyone's feet are unique!)

**What if I order wrong size?**
No problem! Free exchanges within 60 days. We'll ship correct size as soon as we receive wrong size back. See "Returns & Exchanges" article for details.

ARTICLE: PROMOTIONS & DISCOUNTS
Category: Deals | Updated: Feb 2024

**How do I use a promo code?**
Enter code in "Promo Code" box during checkout, before payment. Click "Apply" – discount will show in order summary. Only one code per order.

**Can I combine multiple discounts?**
No, only one promo code per order. However, promo codes DO stack with loyalty points and gift cards.

**Why isn't my code working?**
Common reasons:
- Code expired (check email for expiration date)
- Minimum purchase requirement not met
- Code doesn't apply to sale items (some exclusions apply)
- Code is case-sensitive (copy-paste from email)
- Code already used (most are one-time use)

Still not working? Contact customer service – we'll figure it out.

**How do I get promo codes?**
- Sign up for email newsletter (get 15% off first order)
- Follow us on social media (flash sales announced there)
- Check homepage banner (current promotions featured)
- Birthday month (VIP members get special birthday code)

**Do you have student or military discounts?**
Yes! Verify through our partners:
- Students: 15% off with SheerID verification
- Military/Veterans: 20% off with ID.me verification
Links at bottom of website homepage.

**What about loyalty rewards?**
Earn 1 point per dollar spent. 100 points = $5 off. VIP members earn 1.5x points. See "Loyalty Program" article for full details.

ARTICLE: ACCOUNT & PRIVACY
Category: Account Management | Updated: Jan 2024

**How do I update my account information?**
Log in > Account Settings > Edit profile, email, password, addresses, or payment methods.

**Can I save multiple addresses?**
Yes! Great for sending gifts or if you split time between locations. Mark one as "Default" for faster checkout.

**Is my payment information secure?**
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS). Credit card info is tokenized – we never store full card numbers on our servers. PCI DSS compliant.

**How do I unsubscribe from emails?**
Click "Unsubscribe" at bottom of any marketing email. Or log into account > Communication Preferences > select which emails you want. Note: You'll still receive order confirmations and shipping updates.

**Can I delete my account?**
Yes. Email privacy@athleticfootwear.com with "Delete My Account" in subject line. We'll process within 7 days. Note: This is permanent and can't be undone.

**Do you sell my information?**
Never. We don't sell personal data. See Privacy Policy for full details on how we use and protect your information.
