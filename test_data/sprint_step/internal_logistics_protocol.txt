=== WAREHOUSE OPERATIONS ===

Receiving Process:
- All shipments from vendors must be logged in WMS within 2 hours of arrival
- Quality inspection required for 15% of each batch (random sampling)
- Damaged goods protocol: photograph, report to vendor, quarantine location B-7
- Standard processing time: 24-48 hours from dock to inventory availability

Inventory Zones:
- Zone A: High-velocity items (running shoes, training shoes) - Daily replenishment
- Zone B: Seasonal products (cleats, specialty footwear) - Weekly cycle count
- Zone C: Returns processing - 72-hour turnaround target
- Zone D: Clearance and discontinued models - Monthly audit

Picking & Packing Standards:
- Priority orders (expedited): Pick within 2 hours
- Standard orders: Pick within 12 hours
- Box size optimization required - use smallest box that fits with 2" padding
- Tissue paper wrapping mandatory for premium models ($150+)
- Include promotional inserts based on customer segment (see Marketing guidelines)

Shipping Cutoff Times:
- Same-day processing: Orders placed before 2 PM EST
- Next-day shipping availability: Orders confirmed before 6 PM EST
- Weekend orders: Processed Monday morning (first priority)

Returns Handling:
- Inspect for wear, original packaging, tags attached
- Grade A (resellable): Return to inventory within 24 hours
- Grade B (minor box damage): Move to clearance
- Grade C (worn/damaged): Document and dispose per policy

Performance Metrics:
- Pick accuracy target: 99.5%
- Ship-same-day rate: >80% of eligible orders
- Return processing time: <72 hours average
- Inventory accuracy: 98% or higher (monthly audit)
