RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY - Version 1.8
Effective: March 2022 - September 2023
Status: SUPERSEDED - For reference only

RETURN POLICY:
Customers may return unworn, unwashed merchandise within 45 days of purchase date with original receipt and packaging. Shoes must have all original tags attached and be in resaleable condition.

Return Methods:
1. Mail returns: Customer pays return shipping ($8.95 flat rate)
2. In-store returns: Not available (online only business)

Refund Processing:
- Refunds issued to original payment method within 10-14 business days
- Store credit option: Issued immediately upon receipt
- Return shipping costs are non-refundable

EXCHANGE POLICY:
Exchanges for different size or color accepted within 45-day window. Customer must pay shipping costs both ways ($8.95 each direction). Exchanges processed as return + new order.

EXCLUSIONS:
- Sale items marked "Final Sale" (25% off or more) cannot be returned
- Clearance items (50% off or more) are final sale
- Custom or personalized footwear: No returns
- Items without original packaging: Reduced refund (30% restocking fee)

DEFECTIVE MERCHANDISE:
Manufacturing defects covered for 60 days. Customer must provide photos. Prepaid return label issued after review. Replacement shipped after defective item received (7-10 day process).

LATE RETURNS:
Returns received after 45-day window may be rejected or accepted for store credit only at manager discretion (case-by-case).

RESTOCKING FEES:
Returns with missing components (insoles, extra laces, boxes): $15 restocking fee
Premium models ($200+): $25 restocking fee if returned after 30 days

POLICY CHANGES:
This policy may change without notice. Check website for current version.

Note: This version (1.8) was replaced by version 2.1 in October 2023 with significant improvements to customer experience.
