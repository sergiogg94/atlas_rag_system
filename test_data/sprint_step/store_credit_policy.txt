STORE CREDIT POLICY
Athletic Footwear E-Commerce LLC
Effective Date: January 2024

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW:
Store credit is a form of payment that can be used toward future purchases on our website. This policy explains how store credit is issued, redeemed, and managed.

1. HOW TO RECEIVE STORE CREDIT

Store credit may be issued for the following reasons:

a) Returns
   - When processing a return, you may choose store credit instead of refund to original payment method
   - Benefit: Store credit is issued INSTANTLY (no waiting for bank processing)
   - Bonus: 5% extra credit (e.g., $100 return = $105 store credit)
   - See Return Policy for eligibility requirements

b) Exchanges
   - If exchanged item is lower in price than original, difference issued as store credit
   - Example: Original purchase $120, exchange item $100 → $20 store credit

c) Order Issues/Errors
   - Damaged items received
   - Incorrect items shipped
   - Significant shipping delays (at our discretion)
   - Customer service may issue store credit as resolution or goodwill gesture

d) Referral Program
   - When referred friend completes first purchase, you receive $20 store credit
   - Unlimited referrals (see Promotions Policy for details)

e) Loyalty Rewards
   - Redeem loyalty points for store credit (100 points = $5 credit)
   - See Loyalty Program for full terms

f) Promotions & Contests
   - Winner prizes or promotional giveaways may include store credit
   - Terms specified in individual promotion

g) Gift Returns
   - Gift recipient can return for store credit without receipt
   - Amount based on current sale price if original price cannot be determined

2. STORE CREDIT BALANCE & TRACKING

- Store credit balance visible in your account under "My Account > Store Credit"
- Email confirmation sent each time credit is issued or redeemed
- Minimum redeemable amount: $1.00 (no maximum)
- Balance never expires (see Expiration section below)

3. HOW TO USE STORE CREDIT

Redeeming at Checkout:
1. Add items to cart as usual
2. Proceed to checkout
3. On payment page, you'll see "Store Credit Available: $XX.XX"
4. Check box to "Apply Store Credit"
5. Credit will be applied to order total (after discounts, before taxes/shipping)
6. If order exceeds credit balance, pay remainder with another payment method
7. If credit exceeds order total, remaining balance stays in your account

Important Notes:
- Store credit applies to product cost only (not shipping or taxes, unless credit covers full order)
- Cannot be used to purchase gift cards
- Can be combined with promo codes, loyalty points, and other discounts
- Partial use allowed – unused portion remains in account

4. REFUNDS & EXCHANGES WITH STORE CREDIT

If you return or exchange an item purchased with store credit:
- Store credit used for that item will be returned to your credit balance
- Example: $50 credit + $50 credit card = $100 purchase → Full return = $50 back to store credit, $50 to credit card

5. STORE CREDIT EXPIRATION

Store credit does NOT expire under normal circumstances.

However, credit may be forfeited if:
- Account is closed or terminated for violation of Terms of Service
- Account is inactive for 3+ years (no logins, purchases, or activity) – we'll email warning before forfeiture
- Credit was issued fraudulently or obtained through policy violation

6. TRANSFERABILITY

Store credit is NON-TRANSFERABLE:
- Tied to your account (email address) and cannot be transferred to another person
- Cannot be sold, auctioned, or exchanged for cash
- Cannot be divided between multiple accounts
- Exception: If you close your account, credit can be transferred to new account with same name/email (contact customer service)

7. CASH VALUE & REDEMPTION

Store credit has NO CASH VALUE:
- Cannot be redeemed for cash or check
- Cannot be used at third-party retailers or partner stores (online use only)
- If account is closed, credit is forfeited (not refunded in cash)

8. SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES

Account Closure:
- If you voluntarily close your account, unused store credit is forfeited
- You'll receive email warning about credit balance before account deletion is finalized
- Suggestion: Use credit before closing account or request account suspension instead

Deceased Customer:
- Estate representatives may contact customer service to use deceased customer's store credit for final purchases (verification required)
- Cannot transfer credit balance to family member accounts

Fraudulent Activity:
- If we detect fraudulent use of store credit (e.g., from fraudulent returns, stolen account), we reserve the right to:
  - Freeze or revoke store credit balance
  - Cancel orders placed with fraudulent credit
  - Suspend or terminate account
  - Pursue legal action

9. CUSTOMER SERVICE & DISPUTES

Questions about your store credit balance?
- Check balance: Log into account > "My Account" > "Store Credit"
- Transaction history: View all credit issued and redeemed in account dashboard

Discrepancy in balance?
Contact customer service immediately:
- Email: support@athleticfootwear.com (include order numbers and details)
- Live Chat: 24/7 on website
- Phone: 1-800-786-7463

We'll investigate and resolve within 2-3 business days.

10. POLICY CHANGES

We reserve the right to modify this Store Credit Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on our website with "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of store credit after policy changes constitutes acceptance.

Existing store credit balances are subject to policy in effect at time of redemption (not at time of issuance).
