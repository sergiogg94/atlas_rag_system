CARRIER SERVICE AGREEMENT
Agreement ID: CSA-2024-USPS-001
Carrier: United States Postal Service (USPS)
Customer: Athletic Footwear E-Commerce LLC
Effective: January 15, 2024 - January 14, 2025

SCOPE OF SERVICES:
Company appoints USPS as primary carrier for parcel delivery services, including:
- Priority Mail (1-3 business days)
- Priority Mail Express (overnight to 2-day)
- First-Class Package Service (2-5 business days)
- Parcel Select Ground (2-8 business days, cost-saver option)

VOLUME COMMITMENTS & DISCOUNTS:

Projected Monthly Volume: 15,000-20,000 packages
Average Package Weight: 2.5 lbs
Average Dimensions: 14" x 10" x 5"

Negotiated Discounts (from retail rates):
- Priority Mail: 35% discount
- Priority Mail Express: 28% discount  
- First-Class Package: 40% discount
- Parcel Select Ground: 45% discount

Additional Incentives:
- Volume bonus: Additional 2% discount if monthly volume exceeds 22,000 packages
- On-time delivery guarantee: Credit issued for packages not delivered within promised window (Priority Express only)

SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLAs):

Priority Mail Express:
- Overnight to 2-day delivery to most US addresses
- Guarantee: 95% on-time delivery rate
- Tracking updates: Every scan event, real-time
- Insurance included: Up to $100

Priority Mail:
- 1-3 business day delivery  
- Target: 93% on-time delivery
- Tracking included
- Insurance: Up to $50 included; additional available

First-Class Package:
- 2-5 business day delivery
- Best value for lightweight items under 1 lb
- Tracking included

OPERATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:

Pickup Services:
- Daily pickup at Company warehouse: Monday-Saturday, 4:00 PM EST
- No pickup fees (waived due to volume)
- Backup pickup window: Before 6:00 PM if needed

Shipping Label Requirements:
- Electronic manifest submission required by 2:00 PM daily
- USPS-approved label format (4x6 thermal labels)
- Intelligent Mail Barcode (IMB) required on all labels
- Return address and customer service contact mandatory

Packaging Standards:
- Company responsible for packaging integrity
- Boxes must meet USPS dimensional standards
- Fragile items: Additional "Fragile" sticker recommended (provided by USPS)

TRACKING & VISIBILITY:

Tracking Features Included:
- Scan events: Acceptance, in-transit, out-for-delivery, delivered
- Delivery confirmation with signature available (add-on service)
- Photo on delivery (Priority packages to residential addresses)
- SMS/email notifications to recipients

Data Integration:
- API access for real-time tracking queries
- Daily manifest and delivery reports via SFTP
- Exception alerts (failed delivery attempts, address issues)

CLAIMS & LIABILITY:

Insurance Coverage:
- Included insurance per service level (see above)
- Additional insurance available: $1.50 per $100 declared value
- Claims must be filed within 60 days of ship date

Claim Process:
1. Company submits claim via USPS online portal
2. Required documentation: Receipt, proof of value, photos of damage (if applicable)
3. USPS investigates within 10 business days
4. Payment issued within 7 days of approved claim

Lost Package Protocol:
- Priority Mail: Report after 7 days from ship date
- First-Class/Parcel Select: Report after 14 days  
- USPS conducts trace; result within 7 business days

Damage Claims:
- Inspect package within 24 hours of delivery
- Photograph all sides of box + contents
- Submit claim with supporting docs
- Approval rate target: 90% for valid claims

LIABILITY LIMITATIONS:
- Maximum liability: Insured value or $100 (whichever greater)
- Consequential damages excluded
- Company responsible for proper packaging

BILLING & PAYMENT:

Billing Cycle: Weekly billing, invoices issued Mondays
Payment Terms: Net 15 days via ACH transfer
Postage Account: Company maintains prepaid postage account; auto-refill at $5,000 balance

Rate Changes:
- USPS may adjust rates annually (typically January)
- Negotiated discounts apply to new base rates
- 45 days notice required for rate changes

PERFORMANCE METRICS & REPORTING:

Monthly Reports Provided:
- On-time delivery percentage by service level
- Average transit time by destination zone
- Exception summary (failed deliveries, returns, damage)
- Cost analysis and savings report

Quarterly Business Reviews:
- Performance against SLAs
- Volume trends and projections
- Opportunities for optimization
- Service level adjustments if needed
