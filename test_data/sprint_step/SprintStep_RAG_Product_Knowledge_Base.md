# COMMON CUSTOMER QUESTIONS

Q: Are SprintStep shoes waterproof?
A: Only TerraGrip models are water-resistant. No model is fully waterproof.

Q: Can I use Velocity Pro 3 for trail running?
A: No. It is designed for road running only.

Q: What is the free shipping minimum?
A: $120 before taxes.

Q: Is Klarna available?
A: Yes, for U.S. customers only.

Q: Can I return worn shoes?
A: No. Shoes must be unworn and in original packaging.
