MARKETING TEAM - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Quick Reference Guide - Q1 2024

Q: What's our current positioning?
A: "Performance meets style - athletic footwear for every athlete, every day."

Q: Who's our target demographic?
A: Primary: Ages 18-45, active lifestyle, household income $50K+
   Secondary: Parents buying for teens, fitness enthusiasts, casual athletes

Q: How often do we run sales promotions?
A: Major sales: 4x per year (Spring, Summer, Back-to-School, Holiday)
   Flash sales: Monthly, typically first weekend
   Email exclusives: Bi-weekly for loyalty members

Q: What's our social media strategy?
A: Instagram & TikTok focus - user-generated content, athlete partnerships
   Post frequency: 5x weekly IG, 3x weekly TikTok, daily Stories
   Hashtags: #RunYourWay #TrainBetter #AthleticFootwearCo

Q: Influencer collaboration guidelines?
A: Micro-influencers (10K-100K): Product exchange + 15% commission
   Macro-influencers (100K+): Negotiated flat fee + product
   Athletes/ambassadors: Separate contract, contact partnerships@company.com

Q: Email campaign best practices?
A: Subject line: Under 50 characters, emoji use OK
   Send time: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM or 7 PM EST
   Segmentation required: New customers, VIP, inactive (90+ days)

Q: How do we handle negative reviews?
A: Respond within 24 hours, offer solution, take conversation offline
   Never delete (except spam/profanity), showcase transparency

Q: Content calendar access?
A: Shared drive: Marketing > Content Planning > 2024 Calendar
   Submit requests 3 weeks in advance

Q: Brand voice guidelines?
A: Energetic, authentic, inclusive - avoid jargon, speak like a coach/friend
   Style guide: Brand folder > Voice_and_Tone_Guide_v3.pdf
