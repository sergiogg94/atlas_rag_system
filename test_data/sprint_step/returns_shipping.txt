Returns, Shipping, Payments & Service Policy
Version: 2.1
Last Updated: March 15, 2026

----------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — ORDER PROCESSING
----------------------------------------

Orders are processed Monday through Friday, excluding U.S. federal holidays.

Cut-off time for same-day processing: 5:00 PM EST.
Orders placed after 5:00 PM EST are processed the next business day.

During peak season (November 15 – December 31), processing time may increase to 2–3 business days.

Pre-orders:
Products labeled as "Pre-Order" ship on the release date specified on the product page.

----------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — SHIPPING POLICY
----------------------------------------

Domestic Shipping (USA):
- Standard Shipping: 3–7 business days
- Express Shipping: 2 business days
- Overnight Shipping: 1 business day

Alaska & Hawaii:
- Standard Shipping: 5–10 business days
- Express not available.

Canada:
- Standard International: 5–10 business days

Free Shipping Policy:
- Free standard shipping applies to orders over $100 USD (before tax).
- Orders under $100 incur a $9.99 shipping fee.

IMPORTANT:
Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed unless Overnight Shipping is selected.

Carrier partners:
- UPS
- FedEx
- USPS

----------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — PAYMENT METHODS
----------------------------------------

Accepted payment methods:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay

Payments are charged immediately upon order confirmation.

Fraud Prevention:
SprintStep reserves the right to request additional identity verification for high-value orders (above $500).

----------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — RETURNS
----------------------------------------

Return Window:
Customers may return products within 30 calendar days of delivery.

Holiday Extended Returns:
Orders placed between November 20 and December 24 may be returned until January 31.

Return Conditions:
- Shoes must be unworn.
- Original box must be included.
- No visible sole wear.
- Proof of purchase required.

Non-Returnable Items:
- Final Sale items
- Gift Cards
- Customized sneakers
- Worn shoes

Return Shipping Fee:
- $7.50 deducted from refund (U.S. returns only).
- Free return shipping for exchanges.

Refund Processing:
Refunds are issued within 5–10 business days after warehouse inspection.

Refund Method:
Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

----------------------------------------
SECTION 5 — EXCHANGES
----------------------------------------

Size exchanges are allowed once per order.

If the requested size is unavailable:
- Customer may select a different model.
- Or receive a full refund.

Exchanges ship within 3-5 business days after return inspection.

----------------------------------------
SECTION 6 — WARRANTY
----------------------------------------

Standard Warranty:
6-month limited warranty from delivery date.

Covers:
- Manufacturing defects
- Sole separation
- Stitching failures
- Structural midsole defects

Does NOT cover:
- Normal wear and tear
- Improper washing
- Damage caused by sports outside intended use
- Cosmetic damage

Extended Warranty:
Customers may purchase a 12-month extended warranty for $14.99 at checkout.

----------------------------------------
SECTION 7 — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
----------------------------------------

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders may be canceled within 1 hour of purchase.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Only to Canada at this time.

Q: What happens if my package is lost?
A: Customers must contact support within 7 days of the expected delivery date.

Q: Are refunds instant?
A: No. Refunds take 5–10 business days after inspection.

Q: Is free shipping available?
A: Yes, on orders over $120 before taxes.
