VENDOR SUPPLY AGREEMENT
Agreement No: VSA-2024-089
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Term: 24 months (renewal option)

PARTIES:
Supplier: [Vendor Name] ("Supplier")
Purchaser: Athletic Footwear E-Commerce LLC ("Company")

RECITALS:
WHEREAS, Company operates an e-commerce platform specializing in athletic footwear;
WHEREAS, Supplier manufactures and distributes athletic footwear products;
WHEREAS, both parties desire to establish terms for ongoing supply relationship;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of mutual covenants herein:

ARTICLE 1 - PRODUCTS & ORDERING

1.1 Product Catalog: Supplier shall provide Company with access to current product catalog, updated quarterly. Catalog shall include: SKU numbers, wholesale pricing, size availability, minimum order quantities (MOQ), and lead times.

1.2 Ordering Process:
- Purchase orders submitted via Supplier Portal or EDI integration
- Order confirmation required within 24 business hours
- Minimum order value: $5,000 per purchase order
- Payment terms: Net 45 days from delivery date

1.3 Lead Times:
- In-stock items: 7-10 business days to Company warehouse
- Pre-order/seasonal items: 60-90 days from order placement
- Rush orders (if available): 3-5 business days, subject to 15% surcharge

ARTICLE 2 - PRICING & PAYMENT

2.1 Wholesale Pricing: Supplier agrees to provide Company with wholesale pricing at [40-45%] off MSRP, based on volume tiers:
- Tier 1: $50K-$100K annual: 40% off MSRP
- Tier 2: $100K-$250K annual: 42% off MSRP  
- Tier 3: $250K+ annual: 45% off MSRP

2.2 Price Guarantees: Pricing locked for 6-month periods. Price increases require 60 days written notice.

2.3 Payment Terms: Net 45 days. Early payment discount: 2% if paid within 10 days.

2.4 Freight: FOB Destination - Supplier pays shipping to Company warehouse. Expedited shipping costs (if requested by Company) borne by Company.

ARTICLE 3 - QUALITY ASSURANCE

3.1 Standards: All products must meet Company quality standards (QA document QS-2024 attached). Defect rate not to exceed 1.5% per shipment.

3.2 Inspection Rights: Company reserves right to inspect up to 20% of each shipment. Rejected goods returned at Supplier expense.

3.3 Warranty: Supplier warrants products free from manufacturing defects for 12 months from date of manufacture.

3.4 Defective Product Protocol: 
- Company notifies Supplier within 14 days of discovering defect
- Supplier issues RMA and prepaid return label within 48 hours
- Replacement units shipped within 7 days of receiving defective goods
- Credits issued for unrecoverable defective inventory

ARTICLE 4 - RETURNS & STOCK BALANCING

4.1 Seasonal Returns: Company may return up to 15% of seasonal purchase value for overstock items at end of season. Return period: 30 days after season end.

4.2 Return Conditions: Products must be new, unsold, in original packaging. Credit issued at 80% of wholesale cost.

4.3 Stock Rotation: Supplier agrees to accept slow-moving inventory returns (SKUs with <2 units sold per month over 120-day period) up to 5% of total annual purchases.

ARTICLE 5 - MARKETING & BRAND SUPPORT

5.1 Marketing Materials: Supplier provides product images, descriptions, videos at no cost. Company has license to use for marketing purposes.

5.2 Cooperative Marketing: Supplier contributes 2% of gross purchases toward joint marketing initiatives (e.g., email campaigns, social media, influencer partnerships).

5.3 Exclusivity: Non-exclusive agreement. Company may source similar products from other suppliers.

ARTICLE 6 - TERM & TERMINATION

6.1 Term: 24 months from effective date. Auto-renews for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides 90 days written notice.

6.2 Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate with 30 days notice if other party breaches material term and fails to cure within 15 days.

6.3 Termination Effects: Outstanding orders fulfilled; payment obligations survive termination.

ARTICLE 7 - MISCELLANEOUS

7.1 Governing Law: State of Delaware
7.2 Dispute Resolution: Binding arbitration per AAA Commercial Rules
7.3 Confidentiality: Terms of this agreement confidential
7.4 Force Majeure: Standard force majeure provisions apply
