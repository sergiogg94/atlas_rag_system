PURPOSE:
This policy outlines the terms and conditions for promotional offers, discount codes, coupons, and special deals offered by Athletic Footwear E-Commerce LLC through various channels (website, email, social media, partner sites).

GENERAL TERMS:

1. Eligibility
   - Promotions are valid for customers shipping to U.S. addresses only
   - Some promotions may be limited to new customers, VIP members, or specific customer segments
   - Employees and immediate family members of Athletic Footwear E-Commerce LLC are not eligible for promotions unless otherwise stated
   - One promotional code per order unless explicitly stated otherwise

2. Code Activation
   - Promo codes must be entered at checkout in the designated "Promo Code" field
   - Codes are case-sensitive; copy-paste from email or promotional material to avoid errors
   - Code must be applied before completing payment
   - If code does not apply or shows an error, contact customer service before completing order

3. Expiration & Validity
   - All promotional codes have an expiration date/time (usually stated in promotional material)
   - Once expired, codes cannot be retroactively applied to past orders
   - We do not honor expired codes or extend expiration dates
   - Codes are valid for the specified time period only (e.g., "valid Feb 1-7" means 12:01 AM Feb 1 through 11:59 PM Feb 7, EST)

4. Non-Transferability
   - Promotional codes are non-transferable and may not be sold, auctioned, or traded
   - Codes sent to specific email addresses or customer accounts are for that recipient's use only
   - Unauthorized distribution or public posting of single-use codes violates this policy

5. No Cash Value
   - Promotional discounts have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for cash or store credit
   - Discounts apply to product price only, not to shipping, taxes, or other fees (unless stated otherwise)
   - If order is canceled or returned, only the amount actually paid will be refunded (not the "full price" before discount)

TYPES OF PROMOTIONS:

A. Percentage Discounts (e.g., "20% Off")
   - Applied to eligible items before taxes and shipping
   - May have minimum purchase requirements (e.g., "20% off orders $100+")
   - Certain brands or product categories may be excluded (clearly stated in promo terms)
   - Sale items, clearance items, or "Final Sale" products may be excluded unless promo specifies "sitewide"

B. Dollar-Amount Discounts (e.g., "$15 Off $75")
   - Minimum purchase amount must be met BEFORE discount is applied (excluding taxes and shipping)
   - If order total falls below minimum after discount, code will not apply
   - Cannot be combined with other dollar-amount codes

C. Free Shipping Offers
   - Applies to standard shipping only (upgrades to expedited shipping will incur additional charge)
   - May have minimum purchase requirement
   - Does not apply to international or special handling (e.g., Alaska/Hawaii may have restrictions)
   - Cannot be combined with other free shipping offers

D. Buy One Get One (BOGO)
   - "BOGO 50%" means second item (of equal or lesser value) is 50% off
   - "BOGO Free" means second item (of equal or lesser value) is free
   - Discount applies automatically when qualifying items are in cart
   - If items are different prices, discount applies to lower-priced item
   - Both items must be in cart at checkout; cannot be applied retroactively to separate orders

E. Gift with Purchase (GWP)
   - Free gift automatically added to cart when purchase threshold is met
   - Gifts are while supplies last; if out of stock, substitution may occur
   - Gift has no cash value and cannot be exchanged
   - If order is returned and falls below threshold, gift must be returned or charged to customer

F. Flash Sales / Limited-Time Offers
   - Valid for specified time period only (e.g., "24 hours only," "this weekend")
   - Inventory for flash sale items is limited; prices revert to regular after sale period ends
   - No rain checks or back orders at sale price once inventory is depleted

G. Loyalty Rewards & Points
   - Separate from promo codes (can typically be used together)
   - Points earned on purchases (excluding discounts, taxes, shipping)
   - 100 points = $5 reward (may be adjusted)
   - See Loyalty Program Terms for full details

EXCLUSIONS:

Unless a promotion specifically states "sitewide" or "no exclusions," the following items are typically excluded from promotional discounts:

❌ Gift cards
❌ Items marked "Final Sale"
❌ Previous purchases (codes apply to current orders only)
❌ Shipping fees
❌ Taxes
❌ Special order or pre-order items (at our discretion)
❌ Certain premium brands (listed in promo details if applicable)

STACKING & COMBINING:

Can Be Combined:
✓ Promo codes + Loyalty points/rewards
✓ Promo codes + Gift cards
✓ Free shipping offers + Percentage/dollar discounts (unless code specifically prohibits)

Cannot Be Combined:
❌ Two or more promo codes on same order
❌ Promo codes + Employee discount
❌ Multiple dollar-amount discounts

SPECIFIC PROMOTION TYPES:

- 15% off first order when signing up for email list
- Code sent via email upon subscription confirmation
- Valid for 30 days from issue date
- One-time use per customer (based on email address and shipping address)
- Cannot be combined with other new customer offers

Birthday Discount (VIP Members):
- 20% off one order during birthday month
- Code emailed on first day of birthday month
- Valid for entire month
- One-time use
- VIP membership must be active

- Verification required through SheerID platform
- Must re-verify status annually
- Discount automatically applied at checkout when logged into verified account

Military Discount:

- Friend gets 20% off first order

- Referred friend must be new customer (email address not previously used)

Abandoned Cart Offers:
- Customers who add items to cart but don't complete purchase may receive discount code via email
- Typically 10-15% off, valid for 48 hours
- Applies only to items in original abandoned cart
- One-time use

Seasonal Sales:
- Spring Sale, Summer Sale, Back-to-School, Holiday Sale
- Typically 20-40% off select items
- Duration: 7-14 days
- Stackable with free shipping (usually included)
- Final Sale items may apply (no returns)

FRAUD & ABUSE:

We reserve the right to:
- Void promotional codes obtained or used fraudulently
- Cancel orders placed using fraudulent codes
- Suspend or terminate accounts that abuse promotional offers
- Limit quantities per customer on promotional items
- Modify or cancel promotions at any time without notice

Examples of fraud/abuse:
- Creating multiple accounts to use single-use codes multiple times
- Using bots or automated tools to exploit promotions
- Publicly sharing single-use codes intended for specific recipients
- Reselling promotional products for profit
- False identity or payment information

RETURNS & EXCHANGES ON PROMOTIONAL ORDERS:

- If you return an item purchased with a promo code, refund will be for the amount you actually paid (discounted price)
- If partial return causes order total to fall below promotion minimum, discount may be recalculated, and additional charges may apply
- Store credit issued for promotional returns retains the discounted value (e.g., if you paid $50 for item that was regularly $100, store credit will be $50)
